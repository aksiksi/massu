How does it work?

Step 1

You create a Vaulty account and link it to one or more cloud storage services, and optionally configure where and how your email content is stored.

Step 2

We provide you with your own, unique Vaulty email address for each cloud storage service (@vaulty.net).

Step 3

Any email you send to your Vaulty address is streamed directly to the cloud storage service, which means that we never view or store the contents on our side.
