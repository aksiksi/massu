Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Vaulty cost?

Vaulty is 100% free for basic use! There are some limits in place, but you get access to all of the important features found in the Premium tier.

Which cloud providers do you currently support?

Dropbox

Do you keep my emails around?

Never! All emails sent to your Vaulty address are streamed directly to your cloud storage provider. In other words, Vaulty simply acts as a middleman between your email client and your cloud storage account.

Can I create multiple Vaulty email addresses in the same account?

Yes, but only with the Premium plan.

Can I delete my Vaulty account?

Yes, you can do that with one click in your user profile. We scrub out all of your information from the database, so be extra careful!
